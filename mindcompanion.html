About our AI: MindCompanion's AI is designed to support your mental wellness journey, but it is not a substitute for professional mental health care.

AI Limitations: Our AI may make mistakes or misunderstand your situation. Always consult a qualified professional for serious concerns.

Crisis Support: If you are in immediate danger, please contact emergency services or a crisis helpline immediately (iCall: 9152987821).

Privacy: Your conversations and health data are encrypted and never sold to third parties.

About Resolve

We are not a substitute for professional care. If you are in crisis, please reach out to iCall at 9152987821.
